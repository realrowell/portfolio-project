Privacy Notice

Bioseed Careers is committed to protecting your personal data in accordance with Republic Act No. 10173, also known as the Data Privacy Act of 2012.

1. What We Collect

We collect the following personal information when you apply for a job:

  • Full name
  • Contact details (email, phone number)
  • Resume/CV
  • Employment and educational history
  • Other information relevant to recruitment

2. Purpose of Collection

Your data is collected for the purpose of:

  • Evaluating your qualifications for employment
  • Communicating with you regarding your application
  • Maintaining records for future job opportunities

3. Data Sharing

Your data may be shared with authorized HR personnel and hiring managers. We do not share your data with third parties without your consent, unless required by law.

4. Data Retention

Your data will be retained for up to 1 year unless you request its deletion earlier.

5. Your Rights

Under the Data Privacy Act, you have the right to:

  • Access and correct your personal data
  • Withdraw your consent

6. Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

We use encryption, access controls, and secure servers to protect your data.
