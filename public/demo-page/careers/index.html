ABOUT US

Learn more about our company

BIOSEED

Genetics for Improved Agriculture

Bioseed Research Philippines, Inc.

Bioseed is one of the few biotechnology companies which have far ranging expertise across several areas including research and development, field and lab testing, data review, production, farm management and farmer interaction. It aims to empower farmers with high-yielding futuristic solutions, from hybrid breeds to better cultivation techniques.

For further information please visit www.bioseed.com.ph

Established in the year 1992, BIOSEED RESEARCH PHILIPPINES, INC. is today an upcoming company of international repute in the seed industry, with its core strength lying in delivering products that are “performance driven”.

BIOSEED is committed and dedicated to improve the production efficiency of the agricultural community and help provide best possible hybrid seeds to produce quality products.
